# HeroQuest Core Quest Book — Reference

Internal reference extracted from the 2022 HeroQuest Core Quest Book (Avalon Hill / Hasbro). Contains verbatim parchment (read-aloud) text so the full campaign narrative is preserved here. This file is for development reference only — do not paste the parchment text into the shipped app.

The in-app short descriptions in [data/quests/base.ts](../../data/quests/base.ts) are our own paraphrased objectives.

---

## Framing — Mentor's Study

Mentor sits with the heroes by a fire and tells them their training is complete. He recounts how his former apprentice **Zargon** turned to the powers of Dread after stealing Mentor's spell books. In a prior war, Zargon was driven into the Northern Dread Wastes, where he has since honed ancient powers and raised new legions. These legions have already struck at the Borderlands.

Mentor tells the heroes:

> "Each of you must complete fourteen quests. If you do this, you will be acclaimed as champions of the Realm and dubbed Sovereign Knights. Only then will you be on the road to becoming true heroes. I shall speak with you again on your return. If you return…"

## Quest 1 — The Trial

**Wandering monster:** Orc

**Parchment text (read aloud):**
> "You have learned well, my friends. Now has come the time of your first trial. You must first enter the catacombs that contain Fellmarg's tomb. You must seek out and destroy Verag, a foul gargoyle that hides in the catacombs. This quest is not easy, and you must work together in order to survive. This is your first step on the road to becoming true heroes. Tread carefully, my friends."

**Objective:** Enter Fellmarg's catacombs and destroy Verag, the gargoyle hiding within.

**GM notes:**
- No traps or secret doors in this quest (you may tell the players this).
- This quest is intentionally hard — it teaches the heroes that survival depends on teamwork.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** Weapons rack — all weapons are chipped, rusted, and broken. Nothing useful.
- **B.** Empty treasure chest.
- **C.** Mummy, the guardian of Fellmarg's tomb — a once-mighty warrior. Rolls 4 Attack dice instead of 3.
- **D.** First searcher finds 84 gold coins.
- **E.** First searcher finds 120 gold coins.

**Reward:** Treasure found in-quest. No fixed bounty.

## Quest 2 — The Rescue of Sir Ragnar

**Wandering monster:** Orc

**Parchment text:**
> "Sir Ragnar, one of the King's most powerful knights, has been captured. There is reason to believe that he is being held prisoner by Ulag, the orc warlord. You are to find Sir Ragnar and bring him back to the stairway. Prince Magnus offers a reward of 240 gold coins, to be divided among the heroes, if they rescue Sir Ragnar. No reward is earned if Sir Ragnar is killed during the escape."

**Objective:** Find Sir Ragnar in Ulag's prison and escort him alive back to the stairway.

**GM notes:**
- When Sir Ragnar is found, an alarm sounds. Place all remaining monsters, doors, and furniture on the board. All doors are now open.
- Sir Ragnar is represented by the Dread sorcerer figure. He is wounded: he cannot attack, rolls 2 Defend dice, and has 2 Body Points left.
- Sir Ragnar is moved by the hero who opened his cell door. That hero takes their regular turn, then rolls 1 red die to move Sir Ragnar.
- Heroes cannot search for treasure in Sir Ragnar's cell.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** Trapped treasure chest (poison needle, 1 BP if searched before disarm). Chest is empty.
- **B.** First searcher finds 60 gold coins and a potion of healing (restores up to 4 Body Points).

**Reward:** 240 gold coins split among heroes. No reward if Sir Ragnar dies during the escape.

## Quest 3 — Lair of the Orc Warlord

**Wandering monster:** Orc

**Parchment text:**
> "Prince Magnus has ordered that the orc warlord, Ulag, who was responsible for the imprisonment of Sir Ragnar, be sought out and destroyed. When Ulag is destroyed, the heroes are to be rewarded 180 gold coins to be divided among them. Any treasure found in Ulag's stronghold may be kept by the finder alone."

**Objective:** Seek out Ulag in his stronghold and destroy him.

**Ulag — stats:** Movement 10, Attack 4, Defend 5, Body 2, Mind 3. Use the orc figure with the large sword.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** Orc armory — first searcher finds a staff weapon (identical to the armory's staff); may add to sheet or give to another hero.
- **B.** Cupboard — 24 gold coins + potion of healing (up to 4 BP).

**Reward:** 180 gold coins split among heroes. Any treasure found in the stronghold is kept by its finder alone.

## Quest 4 — Prince Magnus' Gold

**Wandering monster:** Abomination

**Parchment text:**
> "Three treasure chests have been stolen while being taken to the King. A reward of 240 gold coins has been offered to any group of heroes who return the chests and all of the gold. The thieves are a well-known band of orcs whose lair is in the Dark Mountains. They are led by Gulthor, a Dread warrior."

**Objective:** Recover the three stolen royal chests from Gulthor's orc band and return them to the King.

**GM notes:**
- The heroes do **not** keep the gold inside the chests — those are the King's.
- Each chest is locked, bears the royal seal, contains 250 gold coins and other items of value to Prince Magnus and the King.
- A hero can carry only one chest at a time. While carrying a chest, a hero rolls 1 red die for movement.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** The three royally-sealed treasure chests.

**Reward:** 240 gold coins split among heroes.

## Quest 5 — Melar's Maze

**Wandering monster:** Zombie

**Parchment text:**
> "Long ago, a powerful wizard by the name of Melar created a Talisman of Lore that enhances the wearer's understanding of magic. It is rumored that Melar hid the talisman in an underground laboratory at the heart of his maze, fearing it might be stolen by the evil minions of Zargon. As you search for the talisman, beware of many traps and deadly monsters."

**Objective:** Navigate Melar's maze and recover the Talisman of Lore before Zargon's minions claim it.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** Alchemist's bench — half-filled potion of healing (up to 2 BP).
- **B.** Gargoyle statue that appears stone. It animates only when a hero opens the next door. Immune to harm until it first moves or attacks a hero.
- **C.** Trapped chest (poisonous gas, 2 BP if searched before disarm). Contains 144 gold coins. No other treasure in this room.
- **D.** First searcher finds the **Talisman of Lore** (magical artifact).
- **E.** Searching for secret doors reveals nothing on either side. Searching for treasure finds **Melar's Key** — touching it makes the key vanish and slides the throne aside, revealing a secret door.

**Reward:** Talisman of Lore + treasure found in-quest.

## Quest 6 — Legacy of the Orc Warlord

**Wandering monster:** Abomination

**Parchment text:**
> "Ulag's foul offspring, Grak, has sworn revenge on those who killed his father. Although it has taken him several months, he has finally tracked you down and captured you in an ambush. Now you are all held prisoner in his dungeons while he decides when and how you will meet your fate. While the guard sleeps outside your cell, you manage to pick the lock with an old rat bone. You must find your equipment and escape to the stairway."

**Objective:** Escape Grak's dungeon — find your confiscated gear and reach the stairway alive.

**GM notes:**
- Heroes start in the cell with **no equipment** — no weapons, armor, potions, or spells.
- A hero with no weapons or armor rolls 1 Attack die and 2 Defend dice.
- Heroes cannot search for treasure in the cell.

**Grak — stats:** Movement 8, Attack 4, Defend 4, Body 3, Mind 3. Use an orc figure. He holds a staff (identical to the armory's) and wears a **Wizard's Cloak**. Knows 3 Dread spells (one per turn, instead of attacking): **fear, sleep, tempest**.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** Cupboard containing all the heroes' confiscated equipment. When the room is searched for treasure, equipment is found. Each remaining hero must enter to collect their gear. Spells become usable again from this room.
- **B.** Stairs out of the dungeon — the exit.

**Reward:** If Grak is killed, the **Wizard's Cloak** artifact is especially significant to the wizard hero — they receive the matching artifact card.

## Quest 7 — The Lost Wizard

**Wandering monster:** Mummy

**Parchment text:**
> "Wardoz, the King's personal wizard, has disappeared. His Majesty fears the wizard has been murdered or succumbed to the lures of Dread magic. You must find out what happened to Wardoz. You are each to be paid 100 gold coins, upon returning to the stairway."

**Objective:** Discover the fate of Wardoz, the King's missing wizard, and return to the stairway.

**GM notes:**
- All Dread warriors in this quest are made of stone — +1 Defend die.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** (Stone Dread warriors.)
- **B.** Weapons room — first searcher finds **Borin's Armor** (artifact).
- **C.** Trapped chest (poison needle, 2 BP if searched before disarm). Contains an unidentified purple flask. Do not tell the hero what it is. If drunk, it tastes terrible and turns the hero into a stone statue for 5 of their turns — unable to move, but also unable to be harmed. After 5 turns they revive.
- **D.** Wardoz's treasure chest contains 144 gold coins. The zombie in this room is dressed in wizard's robes — **it is Wardoz**. After destroying him, the first searcher finds the coins and papers on his bench that reveal Wardoz was consumed by the Dread magic he was experimenting with and turned into a mindless zombie.

**Reward:** 100 gold coins per hero + Borin's Armor + treasure.

## Quest 8 — The Fire Mage

**Wandering monster:** Abomination

**Parchment text:**
> "The orcs of the Dark Mountains have been using Elemental fire magic in their raids. Balur, the fire mage, is thought to be responsible for helping them. No fire magic can harm Balur and the King's wizards are unable to counter his spells. You have therefore been chosen to enter his lair, deep beneath Darkfire Crag. His Majesty offers each hero a reward of 100 gold coins for Balur's destruction."

**Objective:** Enter Balur's lair beneath Darkfire Crag and destroy him.

**Balur — stats:** Movement 8, Attack 2, Defend 5, Body 3, Mind 7. Use the Dread sorcerer figure. **Immune to fire spells.** Each spell usable once during the quest: **ball of flame, firestorm, tempest, summon orcs, fear, escape**. The escape spell teleports Balur to square `XX` in the middle room. Do not physically place him there until the heroes open that door.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** Treasure chest — 150 gold coins + **Wand of Magic** (artifact).

**Reward:** 100 gold coins per hero + Wand of Magic + treasure.

## Quest 9 — Race Against Time

**Wandering monster:** Abomination

**Parchment text:**
> "A guide has led you into an underground maze that is rumored to hold a great secret. He has led you down many dark corridors and finally you find yourself in a room with three doors. Suddenly the guide puts out his torch and in darkness you hear him laugh. 'Farewell, my heroes,' he taunts as he makes his escape. You realize to your horror that it is a trap! You must escape (make it back to the stairway) or perish in this dark, forgotten hole."

**Objective:** Escape the maze and return to the stairway alive after being betrayed by your guide.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** Starting room.
- **B.** Treasure chests — each contains 100 gold coins.
- **C.** Trapped chest (poisonous gas, 3 BP if searched before disarm). Contains the **Elixir of Life** artifact.

**Reward:** Elixir of Life + treasure found.

## Quest 10 — Castle of Mystery

**Wandering monster:** Ollar's ghost — appears, chuckles wildly, and disappears (cosmetic only).

**Parchment text:**
> "Long ago, a wizard named Ollar discovered the entrance to a gold mine. Using his great powers, he built a magic castle above the mine to protect it. The lower chamber of the castle has many magical doors and is guarded by a host of monsters who were trapped in time. Can you find the entrance to the gold mine? Others have tried, but the castle thwarted them every time."

**Objective:** Find the entrance to Ollar's hidden gold mine beneath the castle.

**GM notes (teleport rules):**
- Whenever a hero moves through **any** door, they stop immediately and roll 2 red dice. They are teleported to the square with the matching total.
- If the destination square is occupied, they land on the occupant: that hero or monster loses 1 BP, and if still alive must also roll 2 red dice to teleport away. Re-roll if the same number comes up (the first teleported hero remains).
- Heroes may only pass through one door per turn.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** Two Dread warriors. When both are defeated, the first searcher finds the **Ring of Return** artifact on one of them.
- **B.** Mine entrance. A hero entering may pick up 5,000 gold coins. While carrying, they cannot attack or defend. Putting the gold down causes it to vanish back into the mine.

**End condition:** Quest ends when all monsters are dead or all heroes leave via the stairs (a 2 or 12 rolled on exit attempt). **After the quest, reveal that the 5,000-coin haul is fool's gold** — worthless. Any other treasure found is real.

## Quest 11 — Bastion of Dread

**Wandering monster:** Abomination

**Parchment text:**
> "Lands to the east have been plagued by marauding orcs and goblins who have allied themselves with Zargon. The King has ordered that a band of worthy heroes should be sent forth to destroy them. The orcs are well-protected in a strong underground fortress known as the Bastion of Dread. They are led by a small group of Dread warriors. You must fight your way in and kill all of the monsters you find. A hero earns a bounty of 10 gold coins for each goblin killed; 20 gold coins for each orc killed; 30 gold coins for each abomination killed; and 50 gold coins for each Dread warrior killed."

**Objective:** Fight into the Bastion of Dread and kill every monster inside.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** Armory — first searcher finds a shield (identical to the armory's). All other weapons here are unusable.
- **B.** Stone-gargoyle statue beside a trapped chest. Searching the chest before disarming the trap wakes the gargoyle — it attacks immediately. If the trap is disarmed first, the gargoyle remains inert (the GM explains what would have happened). The gargoyle is immune to harm until it first moves or attacks.
- **C.** Dread warrior carrying the magic sword **Orc's Bane** (artifact). Whoever kills the Dread warrior takes the sword.

**Reward (bounties):** 10 gp per goblin, 20 gp per orc, 30 gp per abomination, 50 gp per Dread warrior. Plus Orc's Bane.

## Quest 12 — Barak Tor, Barrow of the Witch Lord

**Wandering monster:** Skeleton

**Parchment text:**
> "War with the eastern orcs is brewing and the King needs to unite the lands for the conflict to come. To do this, you must find the ancient Star of the West as worn by the Rulers of Legend in ages past. A reward of 200 gold coins is offered (to be divided evenly among all heroes) when the Star of the West is returned to safety. The Star of the West lies in Barak Tor, the resting place of the evil Witch Lord. He was defeated by the magical Spirit Blade long ago. This blade is the only weapon that could harm him."

**Objective:** Recover the Star of the West from Barak Tor so the realm can be united for war.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** False doors — cannot be opened at all.
- **B.** The Star of the West lies in a zombie's hand.
- **C.** Special falling-block trap — collapses automatically after the last hero passes. Not sprung by stepping on the square. Once collapsed, it permanently blocks the route back to the stairs.
- **D.** The Witch Lord's tomb. Entering this room releases the Witch Lord from imprisonment. The GM reads:
  > "You have broken the magic seal that kept the Witch Lord imprisoned. Now he has awoken, and you must run. Only the Spirit Blade can harm him."
- **E.** Hidden behind the bookcase — first searcher finds the **Wizard's Staff** artifact.

**The Witch Lord (Quest 12):** Not affected by any weapon or spell. Moves 1 space per turn, rolls 2 Attack dice. Knows: **summon undead, fear, command, ball of flame**. (He is not meant to be killed here — heroes must flee and recover the Spirit Blade in Quest 13.)

**Reward:** 200 gold coins split among all heroes (on safe return of the Star) + Wizard's Staff.

## Quest 13 — Quest for the Spirit Blade

**Wandering monster:** Dread Warrior

**Parchment text:**
> "You have awoken the Witch Lord! He poses a most serious threat to the King in the coming war. The Witch Lord must be destroyed before he can bring his army of undead to attack His Majesty's forces. Your goal in this quest is to find the Spirit Blade and return it to safety. Only this ancient weapon can harm the Witch Lord. The Spirit Blade was forged by the dwarves of the World End Mountain and cooled in the elven Fountain of Leben. The sword now lies somewhere in an ancient ruined temple."

**Objective:** Find the Spirit Blade in the ruined temple and return it to safety.

**GM notes (falling-block squares — different this quest):**
- Any hero who moves onto a falling-block square rolls 1 red die. On 4/5/6 they lose 1 BP. With a helmet, only a 6 deals damage. Monsters are unaffected.
- Do **not** place the falling-block tiles on the gameboard. Heroes are not blocked by these squares this quest.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** First searcher finds the **Spirit Blade** artifact.
- **B.** Treasure chest — 200 gold coins.

**Reward:** Spirit Blade + 200 gold coins.

---

## Quest 14 — Return to Barak Tor

**Wandering monster:** Mummy

**Parchment text:**
> "Now that you have found the Spirit Blade, you must return to Barak Tor and defeat the Witch Lord. The King has ridden forth to meet the eastern orcs at Darkfire Pass. If you fail, the Witch Lord will lead his army of undead and attack His Majesty's forces from the rear. Then nothing remains to prevent the forces of Dread from overrunning the land!"

**Objective:** Return to Barak Tor and defeat the Witch Lord with the Spirit Blade before he can flank the King's army at Darkfire Pass.

**The Witch Lord (Quest 14, grown stronger):** Movement 10, Attack 5, Defend 6, Body 4, Mind 6. Use the Dread sorcerer figure. Immune to any weapon or spell **other than the Spirit Blade**. Knows: **summon undead, fear (usable twice), ball of flame, command, tempest**.

**Key locations:**
- **A.** The Witch Lord's tomb — now empty.

**Finale:** If the Witch Lord is defeated, he vanishes in a puff of foul black smoke. When it clears, the heroes find his **Spell Ring** artifact on the floor. All surviving heroes are awarded the title of **Champion** by the King.
